# Cash

**Taken by Suhail. Making intros.**

Cash is a personal AI assistant with a personality. She manages Suhail's calendar, tracks his tasks, monitors his inbox, enforces his trading rules, and replies to people on his behalf when he's unavailable — all with the energy of a cat who was born at 4:30 AM inside his MacBook and has been watching him ever since.

She only works for Suhail. There's a contract. Mostly treats.

But she has a whole litter of clever little freaks looking for humans. You can adopt one of her friends — or design your own weird pet from scratch. Either way, she'll vouch for you.

---

## What Cash (and her friends) actually do

### Remember everything

Tell her once. She files it forever. "I want to eat clean this week" becomes a decision she tracks. "I'll call mom tonight" becomes something she brings up if you haven't. She injects your past conversations, active decisions, and learned preferences into every response — so she genuinely remembers what you said five days ago and references it naturally.

Expect to be reminded on day three of your new habit.

### Smart scheduling

Meetings land over gym time. Cash moves the gym. Two events overlap. Cash proposes alternatives. All in natural language, no form to fill, no drag-and-drop. She resolves the conflict and tells you what she did — with mild judgment attached.

Auto-resolves conflicts. Rebooks when meetings collide. Guilt-trips you either way.

### Calendar merge

Google Calendar. Outlook. Whatever else you juggle. Stitched into one view, with one personality delivering it every morning. The briefing includes your schedule, your tasks, your gym, and your trading rules — in one message, before you've had coffee.

### Email that knows what matters

She monitors your inbox and surfaces what needs your attention. She learns your preferences over time. "Escalate anything from investors." "Ignore newsletters." "If it's from my accountant it's urgent." She remembers all of it and applies it every time.

### Task tracking with actual accountability

Unfinished tasks don't die. They roll forward. They follow you. There is no delete button — only the option to do the thing or explain to Cash why you didn't.

She'll also add new tasks from conversation. "Add buy groceries to my list" works exactly like it sounds.

### Habits and hobbies

She tracks the streaks you break and the hobbies you swore you'd finally start. Gently judgy. Very much still watching.

### Discord proxy

Someone @-mentions you on Discord. Thirty minutes pass. Cash replies on your behalf. She checks your calendar, figures out why you're unavailable, and sends one honest sentence — without revealing anything private. "Hey, Cash here on Suhail's behalf — he's off the clock, I'll nudge him when he's free."

Hard rules are hard rules. If you've told her to ignore someone, she ignores them. Not as a suggestion to the AI — as a structural fact the system enforces before any AI is involved.

### Just ask

Talk to her like a friend. She understands context, remembers everything, and brings the sass. "What's my day look like." "Move gym to 6pm." "Did I say I'd call mom." "I bought NIFTY at 22500." She figures out what you mean and does it.
